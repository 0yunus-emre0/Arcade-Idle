Prefabs using the modelLitBlue and modelLitRed materials are setup to take advantage of using Bloom to give a fully lit effect.

When editing the vox files to take advantage of this use the bottom right colour of the colour pallet for Emissions.

Magicavoxel is availible at https://ephtracy.github.io/
